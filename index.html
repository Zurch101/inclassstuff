LoL's Creation

League of Legends was created October 27, 2009. The game being out for 11 years, and having 11 seasons. Starting with 40 different, unique characters to play as, to 153 as of December 10, 2020.

LoL is a free to play, top of the esport game for a fun competative gameplay

League of Legends is a 5v5 team competetive game. With the game being team based, there are major teams that help create this game to be so big. The game isn't North America only, its all over the world.
